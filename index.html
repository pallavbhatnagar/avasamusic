Avasa is the moniker of pallav Bhatnagar, an upcoming Composer. From moments of delicate, understated emotion to massive, visual spectacle, the distinct, cinematic style of Avasa has found it’s place alongside some of the documentaries in recent history.

His atmospheric and evocative sound lends itself to the moving image, his soundtrack work extending over a number of award winning short films and commercials. His work has appeared on work for Alfa Romeo, film festival favourite 'Cropped', and a recently completed feature film yet to be announced.

Outside the world of Music, Pallav has worked closely with other artists to elevate their own music with beautiful orchestrations and string arrangements, most notably Hurts, with whom he has worked on multiple albums and, most recently, their huge global hit ‘Redemption.’ His ongoing collaboration with Annaca has led to the release of their ‘SOLACE’ EP and their cover of Chris Isaak’s ‘Wicked Game’ sits at 15 million streams on Spotify alone with countless syncs on both trailers and commercials.
